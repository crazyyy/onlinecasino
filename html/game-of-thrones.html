The Game of Throne video pokie allows you to play in denominations of 1c, 2c, 5c or 10c per line. You can win up to 10,000 coins in the regular game, 50,000 coins in the free spins or 7,500 coins in the bonus game.

Game of Thrones Symbols

Standard Symbols

The standard symbols of the game include the sigils of the four different houses: House Stark, House Baratheon, House Lannister and House Targaryen. In addition, you will find the usual Microgaming symbols of J (Jack), Q (Queen), K (King) and A (Ace).

Scatter Symbol

The Iron Throne is the Scatter Symbol in Game of Thrones. You will receive a special random Scatter Bonus when the Iron Thrones appears twice anywhere on the reels.

Wild Symbol

The Game of Throne logo is the Wild Symbol and substitutes other symbols with the exception of the Scatter Symbol. It's a Stacked Wild Symbol and can appear more than once on the same reel. Stacked symbols increase your chances of winning big.

Free Spins

The exciting Free Spins Feature in this pokies game is triggered by the Iron Throne symbol appearing three or more times anywhere on the reels. As soon as this happens, you will see a sequence of shots from the HBO Game of Thrones series, followed by a banner picturing the four main houses of the storyline. You will be asked to make a choice:

House Baratheon

You will get 8 free spins with a fixed multiplier of 5x. House Baratheon's sigil has a stack of 3 symbols.

House Lannister

You will get 10 free spins with a fixed multiplier of 4x. House Lannister's sigil has a stack of 4 symbols.

House Stark

You will get 14 free spins and a fixed multiplier of 3x. House Stark's sigil has a stack of 5 symbols.

House Targaryen

You will get 18 free spins with a fixed multiplier of 2x. House Targaryen's sigil has a stack of 6 symbols.

Bonus Game

The Game of Thrones slot game has an interesting bonus game that is actually a gamble feature. You can decide if you want to take your winnings or gamble the entire amount. If you decide to gamble, you're in with a chance of doubling your money. The gamble is a simple choice of choosing heads or tails of a coin. If you win, you will double your money and move ahead on the Game of Thrones Trail Map. You can repeat the process and keep on gambling. If you're lucky, you'll either reach the gamble limit or the end of the trail map. Either way, you will have made a lot of hard cash along the way.

Jackpot

In order to win the jackpot, you will need one or more of the Wild Symbols (The Game of Thrones logo) to show up on each reel. If you manage to have more than one stacked Wild Symbol on a reel, your real cash jackpot win will be even higher: Here you can win up to 121,000 coins if you have been playing the 243 ways of wining option.
